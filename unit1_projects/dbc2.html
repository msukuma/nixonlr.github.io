A paragraph (from the Greek paragraphos, "to write beside" or "written beside")(Khalil Milanes) is a self-contained unit of a discourse in writing dealing with a particular point or idea. A paragraph has 5 types (Br. Anton Heitman). A paragraph consists of one or more sentences.[1][2] Though not required by the syntax of any language, paragraphs are usually an expected part of formal writing, used to organize longer prose.
