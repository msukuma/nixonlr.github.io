Dealing with Conflict

08/17/2014

As I was reading DBC's instructions for this week's cultural blog I felt overwhelmed. I kept asking myself why would I reveal something so personal to the world? Without a doubt we've all felt shame and for most of us my guess is that it wasn't our finest hour. So why would I want to reveal that experience to a group of people I've never met? To be frank and honest, This is beyond my capabilities at this point in my life. I read a few of my cohort's blogs and I commend them for their courage. I just don't feel comfortable doing this. I can not explain it at the moment but something inside me has an extreme aversion to this idea. So I'll restrict this blog to how I've dealt with shame.

I'm somewhere North West on the compass of shame. Sometimes I've lashed out, other times I've withdrawn.
