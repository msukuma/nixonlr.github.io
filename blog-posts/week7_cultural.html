Stereotype Threat

08/10/2014

When you think of the times in your life where you've been the happiest, the proudest, or the most satisfied, which values come to mind?

Happiest: Community, Autonomy, Close relationships, Community, Faith, Peace, Health

Proudest: Commitment, Persistance, Will-power, Inspiration, Strength

Most satisfied: Wealth, Self-reliance, Location, Money

What do I think of peace?

In my opinion, peace is one of the most coveted things in the world. It's possible that we go our whole lives trying to hold on to it or seeking it.

Values in my life

I live up to the values I've list up top except for Faith which I have lost.

What was the last topic that someone asked for your advice on?

A friend of mine asked me what I think she should do with her life. I asked her a question Allan Watts asked his audience during one of his many inspiring talks: what would you like to do if money where no object?

Values and Stereotype threat

I have no doubt that my values will help me overcome any obstacles in my way. With respect to DBC, I am committed 100% and will will myself out of any feelings of inadequacy should they surface. I have already invested to much time and effort to consider giving up.
